Taskmate is a task management application designed to help users organize and prioritize their daily tasks efficiently. With a user-friendly interface, Taskmate allows users to create, edit, and categorize tasks, set deadlines, and receive reminders. AnimeManage

AnimeManage is a web application that provides anime enthusiasts with a comprehensive platform to manage their anime collections. Users can create personalized lists of their favorite anime series, track their watching progress, and discover new titles based on their preferences. The application also includes features such as ratings, reviews, and recommendations, making it a go-to resource for anime fans looking to organize and explore their anime interests.

todoList

todoList is a simple yet effective task management tool designed to help users stay organized and productive. With its minimalist design, todoList allows users to quickly add, edit, and delete tasks while providing options for categorization and prioritization. The application also includes features such as due dates and reminders, ensuring that users never miss an important deadline. Whether you're managing daily chores or work-related tasks, todoList is the perfect solution for keeping your life organized.
